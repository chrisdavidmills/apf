About

The sound of heavy.

Releases by Ba’al, Barbarian Hermit, Battalions, Blind Haze, BongCauldron, Desert Storm, Diesel King, Gandalf The Green, Mastiff, Nomad, Pist, Possessor, RedEye Revival, The Grand Mal, The Hyena Kill, The Wizards Of Delight, Tides of Sulfur, Trevor’s Head, Tronald, Under and Widows.

Independent record label based in Salford, Greater Manchester specialising in heavy metal and hard rock and their subgenres: stoner, sludge, doom, thrash, post-metal.

Founded by Andrew Field during March of 2017.
